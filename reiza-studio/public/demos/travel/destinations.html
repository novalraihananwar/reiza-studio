Destinations

12 extraordinary destinations across two of Indonesia's most iconic regions.

Showing 12 destinations

🌋 East Java

Mount Bromo

An active volcano rising from a 10km sea of volcanic sand. The pre-dawn jeep ride and crater-rim walk deliver one of Southeast Asia's most unforgettable mornings.

1–2 days Moderate
$45–$110
🌋 East Java

Tumpak Sewu

Java's most spectacular waterfall — a wide curtain of cascades tumbling into a deep jungle canyon. The descent to the canyon floor is an adventure in itself.

1 full day Challenging
$25–$60
🌋 East Java

Kawah Ijen

The world's largest acidic volcanic lake glows turquoise by day. Before dawn, electric-blue flames — burning sulfuric gas — erupt from fissures in the crater floor.

1 day (depart midnight) Challenging
$35–$75
🌋 East Java

Malang

At 450 m elevation, Malang enjoys year-round spring weather. Colonial architecture, colourful painted kampungs, and the cool highlands of Batu make it East Java's most liveable base.

2–3 days Easy
$20–$65
🌋 East Java

Banyuwangi

The easternmost regency of Java, where the Ijen crater, Baluran's savannah, deserted beaches, and the ferry to Bali all converge within 90 minutes of each other.

2–3 days Easy
$18–$55
🌋 East Java

Madakaripura

A 200-metre waterfall at the end of a narrow moss-covered gorge. Considered the meditation site of Gajah Mada, Majapahit Empire's greatest general — enter by wading through a shallow river.

3–4 hours Moderate
$10–$30
🏝️ Bali

Ubud

The cultural capital of Bali. Rice terrace walks, Kecak fire dance, the Sacred Monkey Forest, and genuine Balinese Hindu ceremony all coexist in a single valley.

3–5 days Easy
$40–$160
🏝️ Bali

Uluwatu

A dramatic limestone peninsula 70 metres above the Indian Ocean. Home to Pura Luhur Uluwatu, the nightly Kecak fire dance, and Bali's best surf breaks.

1–2 days Easy
$45–$190
🏝️ Bali

Nusa Penida

Bali's most dramatic offshore island. Kelingking's T-Rex-shaped cliff, Angel's Billabong natural infinity pool, and Manta Point snorkelling are genuinely unmissable.

2–3 days Moderate
$55–$170
🏝️ Bali

Tanah Lot

Built on an offshore rock pillar in the 16th century, Tanah Lot is one of Bali's seven sea temples. The sunset silhouette draws thousands — but arrive early for a peaceful experience.

Half day Easy
$12–$45
🏝️ Bali

Kintamani & Mount Batur

Bali's most accessible active volcano sits inside a vast highland caldera beside the sacred crater lake. The 2 am sunrise trek is one of the island's essential experiences.

1–2 days Moderate
$30–$90
🏝️ Bali

Canggu

Where Echo Beach surf meets rice-paddy yoga studios, rooftop cocktail bars, and the highest concentration of great food per square kilometre in Bali.

3–7 days Easy
$40–$175
