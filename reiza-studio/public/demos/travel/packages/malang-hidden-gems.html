Malang Hidden Gems Tour

Colourful kampungs, vintage vehicles, highland waterfalls, and apple country in 2 days

Route

Trip Highlights

Jodipan & Ksatrian — the original painted kampung
Museum Angkut — 300 vintage vehicles in themed zones
Batu Night Spectacular — illuminated gardens after dark
Coban Rondo waterfall — an easy tropical canyon walk
Batu apple orchards — pick-your-own at source

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Start at the colonial alun-alun and Ijen Boulevard — Dutch-era architecture still standing. Walk through the Instagram-famous Jodipan village. Afternoon at Museum Angkut, evening at Batu Night Spectacular.
